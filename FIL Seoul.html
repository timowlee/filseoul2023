    About FIL Seoul

    The upcoming FIL Seoul is scheduled for September 6th to 7th. Presented by the Secured Finance with the support of the Filecoin Foundation, The FIL Seoul Network Base serves as the central hub for a range of activities including engaging lightning talks, immersive workshops, and collaborative coworking sessions. Moreover, it's an excellent opportunity to forge new connections and build meaningful friendships.
